Dr. J. Robert Oppenheimer

Dr. J. Robert Oppenheimer was an American theoretical physicist and the head of the Los Alamos Laboratory during the Manhattan Project. He is credited with leading the development of the first nuclear weapons, a pivotal moment in world history.

Known for his profound intellect and leadership, Oppenheimer is also remembered for his reflective stance on the ethical implications of nuclear technology. His famous quote, "Now I am become Death, the destroyer of worlds," underscores the gravity of his achievements.
